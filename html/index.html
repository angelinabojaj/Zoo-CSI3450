About Us

Get up close with exotic creatures from across the globe, wander through lush landscapes, and embark on thrilling safari experiences. Meet our feathered friends in the interactive aviary, where colorful parrots and majestic eagles soar overhead. Visit the reptile kingdom and come face-to-face with slithering serpents, giant tortoises, and scaly surprises! More than just a zoo, Crazy Critters is a sanctuary for conservation and education, helping protect endangered species and inspire future wildlife champions. Fun for all ages! Whether you're feeding a gentle giraffe, watching a playful penguin parade, or hopping on a zoo train ride, there's something for everyone to enjoy! Plan Your Visit Today! Get ready for a day of adventure, laughter, and memories that will last a lifetime.
